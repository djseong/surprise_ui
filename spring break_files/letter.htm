Dear Mitch:

Its finally here Mitch. I still remember when you first told me you were thinking about going to Africa. I didnt really believe you and I thought you would stay in San Francisco. I thought I would be able to come down to the bay often and we could hang out like last year, except now I dont have to feel bad afterwards. But things happened, and youre leaving for Africa today. You already know but obviously Im sad that youre going so far away. Im sad and worried and I wish I could hold on to you. I wish I could hold you back so that you cant leave my side. But as Ive been thinking more about your trip, Im realizing how selfish I can be. I dont want to put you into this Dan box. Youre far too complex (in a good way) than any box I can come up with. So now, Im still sad and worried, but I want you to know that Im genuinely excited for you. Im genuinely excited to see how God will use this experience to change you in profound and beautiful ways. Ill be here for you Mitch, just the way I was when were best friends, to walk with you in your experiences in Africa. I want to be here for you because you matter to me. I want to continue to develop a close relationship with you so I hope you feel that you can talk to me whenever you are happy, sad, excited, or a little down. Ill try to do the same for you. J

Im also excited to see the new ways in which we will be intimate with each other and how our relationship will grow from that. I think recently Ive been trusting our relationship more because Ive been seeing how grounded it is on God. As I trust more in the relationship, the things that used to make me a little scared or anxious now make me excited. Im less anxious about whether well be able to be intimate during long distance and more excited about how were going to be intimate. I know its possible and I also know we both want to do it. I really think we can so lets not get discouraged by long distance and instead find ways to show Godly love to each other no matter where we are. I still remember my vision for our relationship the light, joyous, and loving feeling. I really feel that were getting closer, and it seems a little ironic that long distance would help us get to that vision but I really think itll help. Lets continue to be honest, speak truth, and above all just walk with each other like the best friends we are so good at being.

Lastly, I want to thank you for being here with me. I know that Ive been unkind, selfish, and have hurt you repeatedly. But you still choose to be here and that means a lot to me. I told you that I feel like youre a gift and I really mean that. Im so thankful that I have a Christian girlfriend like you who is so not complacent. Im only going to say this once, but sometimes I think its good when youre being a mess and telling me about it because it forces me to reflect on where Im at in my faith. You told this to me and I want to say it back weve been through so much together. That morning where I felt God telling me to let go, the way I cried so openly in your arms, and the emotions and visions I received from praying so fervently about you are some of the experiences that Ill probably never forget in my life. Thanks for being here with me through all of them Mitch. Its because of these experiences that I think that youre a gift to me and I feel like we understand each other. So remember that if you ever feel lonely in Africa. I think the cure for loneliness is empathy, so remember that I can/am willing to understand and empathize with you. Let me know if you are feeling lonely and Ill be whatever you need comforter, counselor, or comedian. Not because I have to, but because I want to. J

Have a great two months Mitch. Im so excited to see how God will use this time to change both of us individually and in our relationship. Ill always be praying for you.

- Dan
